I am studying the application of deep learning techniques in the medical domain, targeting the "possibility" of diagnosis. This web application, which does not yet exist, might be available by the deadline for thesis defense. This description will soon be updated with a better explanation

" Supraspinatus tendons "

The supraspinatus tendons are part of the rotator cuff of the shoulder. It is mainly responsible for arm abduction, so patients who suffer from tearing in this muscle would experience difficulty when lifting their arms laterally.

This website, in near future, will contain two APIs for shoulder, which will be explained further in the section below.

Applications

The API page, accessible through the topbar menu on top-right , provides two applications:

1. Shoulder-bone pointer

The application "Shoulder-bone pointer", just like its name, helps pointing out the outline of shoulder bone. The application takes in MRI images as inputs.

2. Rotator cuff tear

The application "Rotator cuff tear" will take in an MRI image, and tell if the patient is suffering from rotator cuff tear.
